========================================
RECIPES
========================================
1) Pesto Chicken Veg Pasta (20 min)
Ingredients:
- Pasta
- Pesto
- Precooked chicken
- Frozen vegetables
- Splash milk (optional)
- Black pepper
Steps:
1. Boil pasta; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. Heat chicken + vegetables in a skillet.
3. Add pesto + small amount of pasta water; toss in pasta.
4. Optional: add tiny milk splash for creaminess.
Storage:
- Fridge 3 days. Reheat with splash of water.

2) Chicken Ramen Stir-Fry (20 min)
Ingredients:
- Ramen (use less seasoning)
- Precooked chicken
- Frozen vegetables
- 1-2 eggs
- Soy sauce
Steps:
1. Cook ramen 2 minutes; drain.
2. Stir-fry vegetables + chicken; add noodles.
3. Push aside and scramble eggs; mix together.
4. Season lightly with soy sauce.
Storage:
- Fridge 2-3 days.

3) Mild Beef & Bean Bowls (25 min)
Ingredients:
- Frozen beef
- Beans
- Onion
- Rice
- Cumin
- Paprika
- Salt
Steps:
1. Brown beef with onion.
2. Add beans + mild spices + splash water.
3. Simmer 8 minutes; serve over rice with avocado.
Storage:
- Fridge 4 days.

4) Mediterranean Chicken Wraps/Bowls (20 min)
Ingredients:
- Chicken
- Cucumber
- Lettuce
- Wraps or rice
- Yogurt (small amount)
- Garlic
- Olive oil
- Herbs
Steps:
1. Warm chicken with olive oil + herbs.
2. Mix yogurt, garlic, and salt for drizzle.
3. Assemble wraps or bowls with cucumber + greens.
Storage:
- Keep sauce separate; fridge 3 days.

5) Chicken Egg Fried Rice (25 min)
Ingredients:
- Cooked rice
- Chicken
- Frozen vegetables
- Eggs
- Soy sauce
- Oil
Steps:
1. Heat oil; cook eggs and set aside.
2. Stir-fry vegetables + chicken.
3. Add rice, return eggs, and season with soy.
Storage:
- Fridge 3-4 days.

6) Sheet-Pan Chicken & Potatoes (30 min active)
Ingredients:
- Chicken
- Potatoes
- Frozen vegetables
- Oil
- Mild spices
Steps:
1. Cube potatoes; season with oil/spices.
2. Add chicken on tray; bake at 425F until done.
3. Add vegetables near the end to avoid overcooking.
Storage:
- Fridge 4 days.
